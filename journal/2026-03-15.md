- Drove Highlander from Houston to Longmont.
    - Oscillation, which is felt not only in
      steering wheel, seems worst between 65
      mph and 75 mph.
    - Driving at 60 mph or slower reveals
      hardly any oscillation.
    - Driving at 84 mph (while in 75-mph
      speed-limit zone) is not so good as 60
      mph in terms of vibration, but it is
      much better than 75 mph.
    - Oscillation seems worse when foot is
      off the accelerator-pedal.
    - Oscillation seems worse when taking a
      rightward bend in the road.
